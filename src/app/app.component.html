Angular in Louisville

Angular is a prominent technology

The Angular JavaScript framework is a very prominent technology, both in Louisville and around the world. According to the Louisville Tech 2017 Developer Survey, over 14% of developers in Louisville use AngularJS, and almost 10.5% of developers use Angular. On a much larger scale, almost 37% (out of 51,620 responses!) of the respondents to the Stack Overflow Developer Survey 2018 stated they use Angular.

These numbers are significant. Considering the amount of people who use Angular or AngularJS in Louisville, it makes sense for those people to have a common place to come and share their knowledge, passions, and experiments with each other. ngLou aims to fill that need by hosting monthly meetups with speakers discussing relevant topics and the opportunity to network with like-minded developers.
